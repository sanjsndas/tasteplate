What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information. Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, TastePlate) are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (e.g., advertising, interactive content, and analytics). The parties that set these third-party cookies can recognize your computer both when it visits the website in question and also when it visits certain other websites.

Why do we use cookies?

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for our website to operate, and we refer to these as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Other cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our Site. For example, TastePlate uses cookies to remember your preferences, such as language settings or login status, making your future visits more convenient and personalized. These cookies help us to understand how our website is being used, allowing us to continuously improve its functionality and content based on user behavior. Furthermore, certain cookies are used for advertising purposes, helping us to deliver more relevant advertisements to you based on your interests and browsing patterns. This ensures that the ads you see are more likely to be of interest to you, making your overall experience with TastePlate more engaging and less intrusive. We also use cookies for security purposes, helping to detect and prevent fraudulent activities and protect your account. The detailed information gathered through these cookies allows us to analyze website traffic, identify popular content, and troubleshoot technical issues, all contributing to a smoother and more efficient user experience. Our commitment to using cookies responsibly and transparently means that we only deploy them for legitimate purposes that benefit both our users and the operational integrity of TastePlate. We continuously review our cookie usage to ensure compliance with evolving privacy regulations and best practices, demonstrating our dedication to safeguarding your information. This proactive approach to managing cookie usage is a critical component of our overall privacy framework, reinforcing your trust in TastePlate.
